Ideate2Sustain

13th April'22 - 17th April'22

A team-based competition on the 17 SDGs where participants were required to ideate and come up with a sustainable solution for an existing problem in the real world.

Delete2Sustain

22nd April'22 - 24th April'22

Every journey begins with the first step. We launched an email deletion campaign keeping this in mind. With the combined efforts, more than 500 people advanced the mission of lowering carbon footprints.

Aware2Sustain

30th May'22 - 02nd June'22

It was launched to spread awareness about Green Computing and motivate individuals to reduce their carbon footprints.More than 600 people joined us in this awareness campaign and began their journey toward a greener world.
